Features

Everything You Need, Built-In

Our all-in-one system handles the heavy lifting so you can focus on your properties.

💳

Rent Management

Track due dates and process Mobile Money payments (MTN, Telecel) instantly. Automated reminders sent to tenants before rent is due.

🛠️

Maintenance Tracking

Tenants report issues with photos. Landlords assign, monitor, and resolve maintenance requests in real-time from any device.

📄

Digital PDF Receipts

Professional receipts automatically generated and emailed for every transaction. No more handwritten receipts or disputes.

💬

Direct Messaging

Built-in group chat per plaza. Landlords can broadcast announcements or message tenants directly — no WhatsApp needed.

🏢

Plaza Control

Organize multiple units into plazas. Track occupancy, lease expiry, and rental history for every unit at a glance.

📊

Analytics & Reports

Visual dashboards showing revenue trends, occupancy rates, and payment history. Export reports as PDF or CSV anytime.

🔑

Invite Code System

Generate secure invite codes per unit. Tenants join your plaza instantly — no manual setup, no paperwork.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Automated alerts for rent due dates, maintenance updates, lease renewals, and payment confirmations for both parties.

🔒

Secure & Private

Bank-grade encryption, role-based access control, and secure authentication protect your data and your tenants' privacy.
